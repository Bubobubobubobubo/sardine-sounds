Dumpster sounds in this folder have no copyright and may be used and distributed freely.
These are field recordings made with a hard marimba mallet on various parts of a public metal dumpster. One sound was made with a plastic windshield scraper. They are particularly resonant percussive sounds that work well together.

equipment: Tascam DR-100, Rode shotgun mic: NTG4 

00 - 05 single strike 
06 - 08 4 strikes
